About Me

I am an AI Research Engineer with a focus on deep learning, computer vision, and NLP for healthcare applications and Low-resource Languages. My work spans AI-powered medical imaging systems, multilingual clinical NLP models, and the deployment of reliable, scalable AI solutions.
